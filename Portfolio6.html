The Event of the Year

Ingles: Imagine there is going to be an amazing sporting event between two of the best teams in your favorite sport. And you are in charge of creating a TV commercial for the event. Write a script.
Congratulations, you are invited to an incredible and unique sporting event, records may be broken in this event and if it is the only one, you have to live it or perhaps... Is it too much adrenaline to be able to bear it?!
Come to the big event and learn to live healthy!



Español: Imagina que va a haber un evento deportivo increíble entre dos de los mejores equipos de tu deporte favorito. Y usted está a cargo de crear un anuncio de televisión para el evento. Escribe un guion.
Felicidades usted está invitado a un increíble y unico evento deportivo, puede que en este evento se rompan records y capaz si sea el unico, hay que vivirlo o acaso... Es mucha adrenalina para poder soportarlo?!.
Venga al gran evento y aprenda a vivir sanamente!!!.
