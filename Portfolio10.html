Fixing It At Home

Ingles: Write a short paragraph about fixing things at home. Read the questions and make sure you include that information in your paragraph.
What do you do when there are problems in your home?.
R= I call a specialist, because it would be more effective.
Do you or anyone in your family fix them, or do you call someone?.
R= We call someone who is specified in that and who is economical.
Do you or anyone in your family like working with tools?.
R= Yes to my uncle, but he is always busy, taking care of my cousins.
What tools do you have in your home?.
R= Kitchen ones, wrench, hammer and screwdrivers.
